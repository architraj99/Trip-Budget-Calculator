# Trip Budget Calculator

A simple  website that helps travelers estimate trip expenses before they travel. 
Users can enter transportation, hotel, food, activities, and other costs to calculate the total trip budget and track remaining funds against a planned budget.

## Features

* Calculate total trip expenses
* Enter destination and traveler information
* Cost breakdown by category:

  * Transportation
  * Hotel
  * Food
  * Activities
  * Other Expenses
  
* Calculate cost per traveler
* Compare expenses with a planned budget
* Display remaining budget
* Save trip information for future reference
* Responsive design for desktop and mobile devices


## How to Use

1. Enter your destination.
2. Enter the number of travelers.
3. Fill in estimated costs for:

   * Transport
   * Hotel
   * Food
   * Activities
   * Other expenses
   
4. Enter your planned budget.
5. Click CALCULATE EXPEMSE
6. and then you can View:

   * Total Expense
   * Cost Per Person
   * Budget Remaining
   
7. Save the trip for future comparison.


5. Wait a few minutes for deployment.
